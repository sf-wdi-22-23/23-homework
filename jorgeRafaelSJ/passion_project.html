Passion Project

I have been thinking for some time about figuring out a way to make classical music more accessible for curious new listeners. Most people are turned off by the "pretentiousness" of the art environment because of how obscure it can seem. I would love to come up with a solution that can show a curious listener that:

  • Symphonic works are not all long and boring.
  • Opera singers, in most cases, are not fat ladies scream-singing while wearing viking hats.
  • Piano music is so much deeper than just fast notes.
  • Understanding musical expression will change one's life.

The ideal way would be to engage the musical community in this process somehow. Maybe by musicians having a way in which they can submit posts for approval. And select a few every day along with a brief and down-to-earth description. The user interface would be extremely crucial to maintain engagement. Having different easy to navigate sections separating classical sub-genres furthermore helping to help the ear focus interest and taste.
